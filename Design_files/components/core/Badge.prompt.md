One-sentence: A small tracked-smallcaps marker for assistant-form tags, weather, phase, and item tags.

```jsx
<Badge tone="brass">CAT</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral">Overcast</Badge>
<Badge tone="corruption">Wrong rain</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | candle | brass | moss | danger | corruption`. Always renders UPPERCASE tracked. Use `corruption`/`danger` sparingly and only when canon allows them to be visible.
