# The Assistant

## Nature
The Assistant is an ambiguous helper that grows from a cat into hooded forms as trust and awareness rise. It never states its intent plainly. Five canonical forms overlap with NPCs in ways nobody can fully resolve.

## Cat Form
Early game, low trust. Brindle, or a strange stray. The smoke is bread, not burning. Probably.

## Wanderer Form
Grey cloak, face in shadow. Whisper arc. Dry, tired, pausing mid-sentence. It knows the road changed before you did.

## Child Form
Draws gears in the dirt. STIRRING anomalies. Small, matter-of-fact about impossible things. Overlaps Wren — nobody is certain which is which.

## Tinker Form
Overlaps Ilya — ambiguous. Precise, careful. Counts you twice: once for now, once for later.

## Reflection Form
Player silhouette in water or mirror. High Awareness. The form it wears when ambiguity thins.

## White Robe
*DORMANT · Mercy / the guide it pretends to be*

Linen-pale, almost saintly. The Assistant at its most reassuring — and least trustworthy. Bright until the line lands wrong.

## Grey Robe
*STIRRING · The wanderer, neutral, watching*

Road-dust grey, face in shadow. Dry, tired, pausing mid-sentence.

## Red Robe
*SPREADING · Appetite / the gears beneath*

Quiet blood, not heraldry. Worn when the Assistant's interest sharpens into hunger. Brass threads catch the firelight at the hem.

## Black Robe
*CONSUMING · The Clockwork Dark itself*

Ironwood black, clockwork filigree at the cuffs. The form it wears when ambiguity is over. Candlelight makes the gears move.
