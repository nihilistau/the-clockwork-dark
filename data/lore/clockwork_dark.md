# The Clockwork Dark

## Nature of the Wound
The Clockwork Dark is not a demon with a name you can banish. It is a metastasizing logic: gears in wheat, brass filaments in nerves, militias marching in perfect time until they march into the sea. It stains the world inward from the Heartlands.

## Signs at the Margin
At Edgewood's edge the signs are small: stillborn lambs with brass teeth, bread that rings when it cracks, clocks that tick hours that never were. Travelers who ignore these signs may live quiet lives. Those who listen may not stay quiet long.

## Tinker Charms
Tinkers sell sympathy charms and ward pins that sometimes work and sometimes merely reassure. Ilya of the Nine Pins trades maps of roads that shift when the wheat turns wrong.
