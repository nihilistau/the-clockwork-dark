# Edgewood Village

## The Margin Settlement
Edgewood is the last comfortable village before the deep woods give way to the Marches. Timber frames lean together around a communal stone oven. Traders pass through twice a season, and the shrine to unnamed saints never lacks a candle — though nobody can name the saints.

## Maris Hearth and the Bakery
Maris Hearth runs the Hearth Bakery with flour on her sleeves and a hum in her throat. Villagers say she hums to keep the gears quiet. She buys wild mushrooms from travelers and sells loaves that taste of honest hunger, not prophecy.

## The Shrine Mural
The shrine wall bears an incomplete mural: saints with missing faces, wheat threaded with brass, a road winding inward. The mural was never finished. Old women say finishing it would invite the road to arrive.
