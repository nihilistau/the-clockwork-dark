# Rumors, Mural, and Phases

## Village Rumors
- (dormant) The militia recruitment board has fresh nails — someone expects volunteers.
- (stirring) Odran swears the road to Millhaven took an extra hour though the sun said otherwise.
- (stirring) Maris burned a batch of bread that rang like a bell when it cracked.
- (stirring) Tinkers are buying old clock parts at twice their weight in copper.
- (stirring) Children in the square drew gears in the dirt; by morning the drawings were gone.
- (spreading) A stillborn lamb was found with brass filings where its teeth should be.
- (spreading) Wheat near the corruption border stands in rows too straight for wind.

## Shrine Mural Fragments
The unfinished mural gains a fragment as each phase deepens. Corruption is told through the wall, not the HUD.
- **dormant**: a saint with clock-hands where eyes should be
- **stirring**: wheat stalks threaded through brass gears
- **stirring**: a child offering bread to something underground
- **spreading**: the Marches road winding into a wound of light
- **consuming**: a village burning in perfect symmetry

## Dormant Phase
Warm linen, moss, honey light. Clean journal; no corruption motifs. The margin still feels habitable.

## Stirring Phase
Brass accents; shadows too long. Subtle tick motif in dividers. Wrong rain may fall upward. Bread may ring when it cracks.

## Spreading Phase
Desaturated greens; sickly chartreuse. Weather widget shows wrong readings. Wheat stands in rows too straight for wind.

## Consuming Phase
High contrast; clockwork filigree. Letterbox cutscenes; UI may stutter one frame per minute. The tower is reachable; the logic keeps its time.
