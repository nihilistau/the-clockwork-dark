# Bestiary

## Forest wolf
*Hungry, not evil*

Lean and rain-wet, ribs showing. The honest danger of the margin — it wants the meat in your pack, not your soul. Flee is a real option. Any phase, birch dusk.

## Militia deserter
*Desperate human*

A man who left Millhaven's gate before it shut. Mud, a notched blade, eyes that already lost. He is not a monster — which is the worst of it. STIRRING phase, the wet road.

## Field scarecrow
*Wrong, standing too still*

It was a scarecrow yesterday. Today it faces the road, and nobody turned it. The first thing the wheat-corruption wears. STIRRING phase, wheat margin.

## Corrupted scarecrow
*Brass nails, wrong shadow*

Brass driven where straw should be, a shadow that falls the wrong way. It ticks. When it moves you wish it had stayed a scarecrow. SPREADING phase.

## Clockwork beast
*Gears in muscle*

Something that was an animal, rebuilt by the logic into a thing of meshed brass and meat. It does not hunger. It only continues. SPREADING phase, the border.

## Clockwork husk
*A clock where the heart was*

Brass ribs, linen tear, a stopped watch where the heart should beat. Not a raid boss — a person the Clockwork Dark finished with. It keeps the wrong hour. CONSUMING phase only.
