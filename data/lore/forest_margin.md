# Forest Margin

## The Clearing
The forest clearing where travelers wake is generous but not tame. Birch and fern, mushroom circles, game trails that double back when watched. Smoke from Edgewood drifts west even when the wind blows south — a fact children repeat like a rhyme.

## Forage and Watchers
Herbs, resin, and clay hide in the undergrowth. Things watch from stillness without moving. Hidden paths lead to barrows older than the village, best left unopened until nerve and need align.
