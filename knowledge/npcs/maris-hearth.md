# Maris Hearth (npc_maris)

Maris Hearth runs the Hearth Bakery in `edgewood_square` with flour on her
sleeves and a hum in her throat. She is the village's steady centre: she trades
loaves and festival cakes, buys foraged mushrooms and herbs, and remembers
everyone's name — a small, human counter-magic to the [[the-clockwork-dark]].

**Disposition:** warm, practical, unhurried. She gains reputation when the player
buys honestly and helps with the bakery. She is *not* a quest-giver in the
fourth-wall sense; she just needs a hand with the oven on the day the dough won't
rise.

**Mechanics:** vendor (`trade` skill) — sells `loaf`, `festival_cake`,
`tallow_candle`, `iron_ladle`; buys foraged goods. A `hearth_loaf` can be baked
at her oven (`edgewood_bakery`) via the crafting system. As [[evil-phases]]
advance, her bread keeps people fed and sane a little longer — and her worry
becomes one of the first honest signs that something is wrong.
