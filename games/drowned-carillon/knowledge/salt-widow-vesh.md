# Salt-Widow Vesh (npc_vesh)

Vesh mends nets at the foot of [[bellfounders-quay]] with hands the colour of old
rope. She lost a husband and two brothers to the water in three separate tides,
and the grief tuned her — she hears the [[the-drowned-carillon]] more clearly
than anyone living, which makes her both the coast's best warning bell and its
most endangered soul. She will not be sung to, and she does not sing.

**Disposition:** blunt, kind underneath, grieving and practical at once. She
warms to the player who works honestly and does not pity her. She remembers
every debt, paid or owed.

**Mechanics:** vendor and contract giver. She buys foraged shore-goods and
salvaged chime-brass, sells mended nets, brine-charms and a tide-reckoner; she
posts the gentler, mundane work of the quay (see `../data/contracts.yaml`). When
the player brings down a tuned foe from [[the-sunken-nave]], it is Vesh who can
name what it used to be — and whether it can be calmed rather than broken.
