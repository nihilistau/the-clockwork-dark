# Brother Cael (npc_cael)

Brother Cael keeps the lamp at [[bellfounders-quay]] and the great shore-bell
beside it — and he has not rung it in eleven years. He learned, the hard way,
that the [[the-drowned-carillon]] answers any bell with one of its own, and that
a struck note calls the water a hand's-breadth higher. So he keeps it silent,
oils the lamp, and counts the tide.

**Disposition:** quiet, dry-humoured, watchful. He gains regard when the player
shows restraint — when they listen before they act, and bring him news of the
[[the-sunken-nave]] without having sung near it. He is *not* a quest-giver in the
fourth-wall sense; he simply needs someone who can carry the lamp oil down the
flooded steps without humming.

**Mechanics:** contract giver and vendor. He posts the work to silence the
Carillon (see `../data/contracts.yaml`); he buys salvaged brass and sells
storm-lanterns and wax earplugs. As [[the-receding-tide]] rises, his refusal to
ring the bell becomes one of the first honest signs that something on the coast
is badly wrong.
